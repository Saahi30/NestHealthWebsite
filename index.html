Can Nest integrate with my existing healthcare systems or electronic health records?

Yes, Nest integrates seamlessly with leading EHR systems like Epic and Cerner through secure, HIPAA-compliant APIs, enabling effortless data synchronization and workflow harmony. This minimizes disruptions, letting you upgrade your existing setup without a complete overhaul, most integrations are up and running in just a few hours.

What key features does Nest offer to patients for better healthcare access?

Patients get 24/7 AI consultations, instant symptom analysis, personalized health monitoring, smart record management, and real-time alerts. This means faster preliminary diagnoses, reduced waiting times, and proactive care, empowering you to manage your health conveniently from anywhere.

How does Nest support doctors in their daily practice?

Doctors benefit from AI diagnostic support, automated patient prioritization, predictive analytics, smart documentation, and evidence-based recommendations. These tools cut paperwork by up to 60%, help identify critical cases instantly, and enable more focused, efficient patient interactions for better outcomes

How much does Nest cost, and what does the pricing include?

Nest is completely free for patients, because we believe revolutionary healthcare should be accessible to everyone, empowering you with AI-driven insights without any barriers


What happens if Nest’s AI makes a mistake with my health information?

Patient safety and privacy is our top priority. While our AI is highly accurate, we’ve built multiple safety layers: all AI recommendations require doctor approval before any treatment decisions, we maintain full audit trails of every interaction, and doctors retain final authority over your care. If an error occurs, our comprehensive medical liability coverage protects both patients and providers, and our 24/7 support team ensures immediate assistance. Remember, Nest enhances human judgment, it never replaces the critical thinking of qualified healthcare professionals.

If Nest’s AI (Barton) is only meant to assist doctors and not replace them, how can it diagnose my disease or act as my therapist and nutritionist?

Nest’s AI assistant, Barton, provides preliminary insights and personalized recommendations based on advanced algorithms trained on vast medical data, but it never makes final diagnoses or prescribes treatments independently, all critical decisions require a licensed doctor’s review and approval. Here’s how it works:

• For Disease Diagnosis: Barton analyzes your symptoms, medical history, and any shared data (like images or vitals) to suggest possible conditions with high accuracy (often over 90% in preliminary assessments). This acts as a smart starting point, like a virtual triage tool, flagging potential issues for your doctor to confirm through exams, tests, or consultations. It’s designed to speed up the process without bypassing professional judgment.

• As a Therapist or Nutritionist: Barton offers supportive guidance, such as evidence-based coping strategies for mental health (e.g., stress management tips) or customized nutrition plans based on your health profile and goals. These are general recommendations drawn from medical guidelines, not personalized therapy or prescriptions. For real therapy or dietary needs, Barton connects you to qualified professionals, ensuring you get expert, human-led care while benefiting from AI’s 24/7 availability.
